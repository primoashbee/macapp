About Us

Triboa Created

The growth of Subic business industry and the volume of workers and employers alike are itself manifestations that there is a great need in medical services. The incorporators of Triboa Medtech saw this need. They believed that health should be a priority especially for rigorous works done in construction, manufacturing companies and shipyards which are the exact industries in Subic has. Named after Triboa Bay (for the sight can relieve anyone who pass by), TRIBOA MEDTECH came about.

Scope of Operation

TRIBOA MEDTECH INC. is a Secondary Clinical Laboratory. It will conduct medical examinations for purposes of pre-employment (local and abroad), serve as health care provider for various companies (i.e. conduct annual medical examinations)and provide other medical services mostly in the diagnostics side.

Our Distinctive Advantage

TRIBOA is consisting of Korean and Filipino doctors. This is to cater the needs of the biggest no. of investors in Subic, which are the Koreans. In this set up, language and culture will never be barriers in the Korean doctor-patient relationship and at the same time, confidence and trust can be easily built even to the other Filipino medical support staffs. This is highly probably since Filipinos are hospitable and friendly in nature.
